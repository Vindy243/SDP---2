About Us

Zer0 Huger is a food waste management system designed to reduce the food wastage and use it in a useful way. We bridge the gap between food waste in abundant societies and food scarcity in vulnerable communities. Our ML model ensures how much food needs to be generated/made at restaurants, hostels, hospitals in order to minimize the waste generated. Even if we have some food waste then, our website acts as a bridge to connect the societies immediately. Resource and Waste management, Social inequity and Technical innovation are what we are addressing to achieve Zero Hunger. It not only reduces food waste but also hunger, which is a major problem of the world. We believe nutritious food should go to people, not landfills. This website is developed by:
